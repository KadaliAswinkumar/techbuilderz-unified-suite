# Pro SEO Delivery Playbook

## Phase 1: Setup (Week 1)
- Confirm target pages and service geographies.
- Add unique title and meta description for every key page.
- Configure Open Graph and social sharing metadata.
- Generate and submit `sitemap.xml`.
- Configure `robots.txt`.
- Verify Search Console ownership.
- Link Google Analytics / equivalent analytics.

## Phase 2: Local and Technical SEO (Week 1-2)
- Create or optimize Google Business Profile.
- Add school schema (`Organization`, `EducationalOrganization`, `FAQ` where relevant).
- Ensure mobile responsiveness and baseline page speed.
- Validate canonical URLs.

## Phase 3: Content Pipeline (Monthly)
- Publish at least 2 admission-focused content updates.
- Update events/news sections with fresh content.
- Refresh FAQ entries based on parent queries.

## Monthly Reporting Cadence
- Track branded and non-branded keywords.
- Review clicks, impressions, CTR, and average position.
- Report top landing pages and conversion actions.
- Plan next month actions with owners and due dates.

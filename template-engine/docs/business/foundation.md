# School Startup Foundation

## 1) Tier Scope, Pricing, and Boundaries

### Basic (INR 2,999 support + one-time build)
- Deliverables:
  - Static school website (5 pages): Home, About, Admissions, Academics, Contact.
  - Responsive design based on approved template variant.
  - School metadata setup (address, timings, facilities, principal message, gallery).
  - One deployment to client domain/hosting.
- Included support:
  - 12 months, up to 2 small content edits/month.
  - Uptime checks for hosted static site.
- Not included:
  - SEO campaign work, database modules, ad campaigns, frequent redesigns.

### Pro (Basic + SEO setup and monthly SEO ops)
- Deliverables:
  - Everything in Basic.
  - SEO setup: metadata, sitemap, robots, Open Graph, local schema.
  - Google Search Console + Analytics setup.
  - Monthly keyword and ranking report.
- Included support:
  - 12 months.
  - 4 content edits/month.
  - Monthly SEO review call.
- Not included:
  - DBMS onboarding, paid ads management, social media campaign creatives beyond defined scope.

### Premium (Pro + Vidyalaya DBMS + marketing collateral)
- Deliverables:
  - Everything in Pro.
  - Vidyalaya tenant onboarding and initial role/user setup.
  - Data migration template + first import support.
  - Staff training (2 sessions).
  - Marketing starter pack (poster + brochure + admission flyer).
- Included support:
  - Priority support with 24-hour response target.
  - Monthly product health review.
- Not included:
  - Custom module development unless separately quoted.
  - Unlimited migrations or daily operations staffing.

## 2) SLA Matrix

| Tier | Response Time | Resolution Target | Monthly Edits | Review Cadence |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Basic | 48 business hours | 5 business days | 2 | Quarterly |
| Pro | 24 business hours | 3 business days | 4 | Monthly |
| Premium | 24 hours | 1-2 business days | 6 + DBMS support | Monthly |

## 3) Onboarding Checklist (All Tiers)

1. Signed proposal + payment milestone confirmed.
2. School intake form completed.
3. Branding assets received (logo, colors, photos).
4. Content bundle received (page copy, admissions, contact details).
5. Approval authority identified (single sign-off owner).
6. Delivery timeline and communication channel shared.
7. QA + UAT walkthrough scheduled.
8. Go-live approval captured in writing.
9. Post-launch support window initiated.

## 4) Payment Milestones

- Basic: 70% advance, 30% at launch.
- Pro: 60% advance, 20% after SEO setup, 20% at launch.
- Premium: 50% advance, 25% after DBMS onboarding, 25% post training/go-live.

## 5) Change Control Rules

- Out-of-scope requests are quoted as change requests.
- Timeline changes must be approved by both parties.
- Third-party costs (domain, paid tools, ad spend) are always client-paid.
